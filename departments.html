Our Departments

Explore the academic programs we offer

Computer Applications

BCA

The Department of Computer Applications offers a three-year Bachelor of Computer Applications program. Students gain strong foundations in programming, database management, networking, and web technologies, preparing them for careers in IT and software development.

3 Years / 6 Semesters 60 Seats 10+2 (Any Stream) University of Kashmir

Key Subjects

Programming in C Data Structures DBMS Web Technology Computer Networks Operating Systems Software Engineering Mathematics

Science

BSc

The Department of Science offers Bachelor of Science programs across Physics, Chemistry, Botany, and Zoology. Students develop analytical and laboratory skills essential for careers in research, healthcare, and applied sciences.

3 Years / 6 Semesters 120 Seats 10+2 (Science) University of Kashmir

Key Subjects

Physics Chemistry Botany Zoology Mathematics Environmental Science

Arts

BA

The Department of Arts offers a Bachelor of Arts program with a wide range of subjects including Urdu, English, History, Political Science, and Economics. It nurtures critical thinking, communication, and cultural understanding.

3 Years / 6 Semesters 180 Seats 10+2 (Any Stream) University of Kashmir

Key Subjects

Urdu English History Political Science Economics Islamic Studies Sociology

Commerce

BCom

The Department of Commerce offers a Bachelor of Commerce program focused on accounting, finance, business law, and management. Graduates are well prepared for careers in banking, finance, chartered accountancy, and business management.

3 Years / 6 Semesters 90 Seats 10+2 (Commerce) University of Kashmir

Key Subjects

Financial Accounting Business Law Economics Taxation Auditing Business Management Statistics
