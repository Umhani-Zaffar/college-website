About Our College

A legacy of empowering women through education

Government Women's Degree College Anantnag

Government Women's Degree College Anantnag is one of the premier institutions of higher education for women in the Anantnag district of Jammu & Kashmir. Established with a vision to provide accessible and quality education to women of the region, the college has been a pillar of academic excellence for decades.

Affiliated to the University of Kashmir and operating under the Department of Higher Education, J&K, the college offers undergraduate programs in Arts, Science, Commerce, and Computer Applications under the National Education Policy 2020.

The college is committed to the holistic development of its students — nurturing not just academic excellence but also leadership, cultural awareness, and social responsibility.

Vision & Mission

The values that guide everything we do

Our Vision

To be a leading institution of higher education that empowers women of Kashmir through knowledge, skill development, and character building — creating graduates who contribute meaningfully to society, the nation, and the world.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, inclusive, and quality higher education to women of the Anantnag region. To foster critical thinking, ethical values, and a spirit of inquiry that prepares students for the challenges of a changing world.
