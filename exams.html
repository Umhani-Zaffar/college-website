Exam Guidelines

  • Carry your college ID card and admit card to the examination hall at all times.
  • Report to the examination hall at least 15 minutes before the exam starts.
  • Mobile phones and electronic devices are strictly prohibited inside the hall.
  • 75% attendance is mandatory to be eligible to sit for semester exams.
  • Any form of malpractice will result in immediate cancellation of the paper.
  • Exam forms must be submitted with required fees before the last date.
